How to make Berry Waffles

To my belief, making my berry waffles are very fulfilling and a very flavourful delight. There're not that many ingredients to make this wonderful meal and quite easy to make.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup frozen berries
  2. 1/8 cup sugar
  3. 1/2 cup Welch's black cherry grape juice
  4. 4 Eggo frozen waffles
  5. 1/3 butter
  6. Eagle Brand sweetend condensed milk /optional

First, we are going to cook the frozen berries. Grab a pot and put your 1 cup of frozen berries in the pot. Then, add your 1/8 cup sugar and add your 1/2 cup of Welch's black cherry grape juice.

Once you add all that in, put berry mixure on stovetop and set it to a medium tempurature (6 or 5) and let it cook for 10 minutes.

After it has been 10 minutes separate the berries from the sauce and let the sauce simmer for 25 minutes.

While you are waiting, you can put the waffles in a toaster and set it to 4.

Grab a 1/3 of butter and melt it in the microwave for about 25 seconds. Then, put the melted butter on the waffles when the waffles are finish

Finally, once the sauce of the berries is done, put the berries and the sauce back together again and put the berry mixure on the waffles

This is optional but, you can add the condensed milk on top of your waffles
